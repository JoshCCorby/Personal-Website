## Sections
- **Lead** – Name, designation, and contact links.  
- **About** – Short description and/or career goals.  
- **Experience** – Previous work experience in chronological order.  
- **Education** – Education details in chronological order.  
- **Projects** – Projects undertaken with links to source/live versions.  
- **Skills** – List of acquired skills and industry knowledge.  
- **Links** – Social media profiles, resume, etc.
